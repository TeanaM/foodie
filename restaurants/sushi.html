Beyond Sushi

Gramercy, Midtown, Chelsea, 229 E 14th St New York, NY 10003

A plant-based, health conscious eatery that celebrates the flavors and beauty of locally-sourced, seasonal produce. Founded by renowned Chef, Guy Vaknin, and his wife, Tali, Beyond Sushi offers an assortment of distinctive sushi rolls and other nutritional staples at their three outposts in Manhattan.

Average Price Per Person: $11-$30
